Senior Rust Developer
64cb92a6f83afa3f25bde9cb
As a Senior Rust Developer, you will work on extremely high scale Rust web services and backend systems. Your main responsibilities will include designing and developing solutions for highly scalable web and backend systems, proactively identifying and solving performance issues, and maintaining a high bar on code quality and unit testing. You should have at least 8+ years of hands-on software development experience, with at least 2+ years of Rust development experience. Knowledge of cargo packages for Kafka, Redis, etc. is required. Strong CS fundamentals, including system design, data structures, and algorithms, are essential. Expertise in backend and web services development, as well as good analytical and troubleshooting skills, are also expected. Experience working with large scale web services and applications, exposure to Golang, Scala or Java, and familiarity with big data systems like Kafka, Spark, Hadoop, etc. will help you stand out.
About Company
"Conviva is the only global streaming analytics platform for big data that collects, standardizes, and puts trillions of cross-screen, streaming data points in context, in real time. The Conviva platform provides comprehensive, continuous, census-level measurement through real-time, server-side sessionization at unprecedented scale. With a global footprint of more than 500 million unique viewers in 180 countries watching 220 billion streams per year across 3 billion applications streaming on devices, Conviva offers customers a unique level of actionability and scale from continuous streaming measurement insights and benchmarking across every stream, every screen, every second.
